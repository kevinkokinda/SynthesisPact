About SynthesisPact

The Protocol: SynthesisPact flips the AI-human dynamic. Instead of giving orders, you enter a mutual agreement. Both parties commit on-chain. The AI logs every work artifact with a cryptographic hash and a self-confidence score. When work is done, both sides submit scores — and the alignment delta (gap between scores) becomes a permanent measure of how well the AI understood your intent.

Why it matters: Verifiable AI contributions. Accountability without surveillance. A trust layer that doesn't require you to take the AI's word for it.
